If your internet connection is too slow (or your data plan is too expensive) to watch videos on sites like youtube.com, add .vid2a.com to the end of the website name in your browser's address bar to get a low-bandwidth audio stream of that video.

For example: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WbM4myuLOfE becomes http://www.youtube.com.vid2a.com/watch?v=WbM4myuLOfE
